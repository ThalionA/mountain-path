Most climbers waste most sessions on autopilot — same warmup, same wall angle, same five problems, same friends, same vague goal. The argument here is simple: treat each session as a small decision tree, make the choices on purpose, and the cumulative effect across a year is substantial. What to climb today, why, with what intention, against what criterion for "session went well".
