A lighter one — me trying to do as many pullups as I can in five minutes. While the video plays uncut, I talk through how to train towards your first regular pullup if you're not there yet (negatives and lockoffs, then assisted with a band or pulley, then unassisted, then progressive overload). The same fundamentals I keep returning to: consistency, gradual progression, adequate rest. If you can already do a regular pullup, the natural next project is [[one-arm-pullup-training|the one-arm pullup]].
