Strength training vs technical learning in climbing isn't an either-or proposition. Strength training isn't meant to replace climbing — it's meant to make the technical learning that happens *on* the wall more effective by removing the binding constraint when strength is the binding constraint. This video sets up the argument for why and when to lift; the follow-up, [[how-strong-is-strong-enough|how strong is strong enough]], answers the practical question of how to test whether strength is still your bottleneck.
