Dave MacLeod is potentially the most well-rounded climber out there, excelling across bouldering, sport, trad, and ice. His third book *Moving the Needle* traces his arc from an average kid at Dumbarton Rock to the first ascent of Rhapsody E11. Four takeaways that stuck: success comes from nailing fundamentals (consistency, effort, showing up) rather than obsessing over training protocol minutiae; do something every day that pushes past your comfort zone; conditions and rest matter more than the discourse admits; and your environment shapes your ceiling (Dave eventually moved next to Dumbarton Rock — it tells you something).
