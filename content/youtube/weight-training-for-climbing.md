The minimalist case: one session per week of bench, deadlift, and squat, plus weighted pullups and max hangs inside the [[warmup-that-made-me-injury-free|climbing warmup]]. That's it. The argument isn't that more weight training would be useless — it's that more weight training competes with the climbing for recovery, and at the grade most of us actually climb at, technical learning is the limiting factor, not raw strength. Later videos refine the argument: see [[role-of-strength-training-in-climbing|the misunderstood role of strength training]] and [[how-strong-is-strong-enough|how strong is strong enough]] for where the diminishing returns kick in.
