## Four noble truths:
- Dukkha: Suffering, frustration, dissatisfaction
- Samudaya: The cause of suffering, which is clinging, attachment, stickiness. We avoid/are blind to the fundamental "anitya" (impermanence) of everything, and we want to avoid staying in unpleasant states and we cling to pleasant ones.  
- Nirvana: The cessation of suffering. Breathing out. Liberation. Awakening. Related to "satori" in [[zen]].
- Marga: Cultivating the path
