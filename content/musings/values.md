Our values define us. They define our actions, but are also revealed by them. They are the only way to navigate the multiple [[everything-is-a-trade-off|trade offs]] that life presents us with. Another way to call values is "priorities", even though the latter has a more practical connotation. 

Here's an unordered list of things I value:
- Presence
- Integrity
- Diligence
- Perseverance
- [[Discipline]]
- [[Stoicism]] (here to mean not complaining)
- Honesty
- Curiosity
- Grace/poise
- Independence
- Courage

Unfortunately, these are not always my guiding principles in life, even though that is what I would aspire to.

A list of their opposites, the things I'd wish to avoid, is also useful to have here:
- Distraction
- Inconsistency/hypocrisy
- Sloth
- Giving up easily
- Indulgence
- Whining
- Dishonesty
- Indifference
- Absence of self-regulation
- Over-reliance on others
- Cowardice
