I'm a postdoctoral neuroscientist working on perceptual decision-making, hippocampal–cortical interactions, and the geometry of neural population activity. Most of what shows up here are notes on individual papers, plus the occasional concept page that ties several papers together.

The structure of a paper note here usually has:
- **Core claim**, in one or two sentences.
- **Methodology** — what they did, what's load-bearing, what's a workaround.
- **Strengths and limitations** — what the result actually licenses you to believe.
- **Connections** — how this changes (or doesn't) my reading of adjacent papers.
- **Personal thoughts** — what I'd do differently or follow up on.

I try not to smooth over contradictions between papers. Where two results disagree, I'd rather say so than reconcile them prematurely.
